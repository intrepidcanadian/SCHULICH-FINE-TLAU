Pop quiz · smart money.

Who picks better loans — algorithmic “robot” investors or human retail investors?
Robots
Humans
Example Institutions plug into the platform’s API and let proprietary algorithms screen every new loan posting in real time.
Result Robots. They systematically outperform less-sophisticated human investors — but only when the portal discloses enough borrower information to gauge default risk.

Does heavy promotional self-praise (no facts) win over sophisticated investors?
Yes, it sells
No, it repels
Example A pitch full of “world-class team,” “revolutionary,” “guaranteed winner” — but light on evidence.
Result No — it repels. Sophisticated investors are turned off by promotional language without factual support. Unsophisticated retail investors, however, are undeterred.
