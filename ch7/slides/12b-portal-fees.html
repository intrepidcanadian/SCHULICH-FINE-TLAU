Origination fee · borrower, on the loan3.0–6.5%

Bank-partner transaction fee · LendingClub1–6%
Early repaymentusually free
But the costs run higher. Customer acquisition alone is $500–$1,000 per customer, on top of technology, R&D, salaries, and G&A — plus funding costs and write-downs on non-performing loans for balance sheet lenders. The result: years of losses, and every publicly listed portal fell below its IPO price.
