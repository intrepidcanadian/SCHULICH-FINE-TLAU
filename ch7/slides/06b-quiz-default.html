Loans for a wedding, a car, or debt consolidation — default more or less?
More
Less
Example Compare them with loans for medical bills or to fund a small business.
Result Less. Borrowing for a wedding, a car, or debt consolidation — or being a professional — predicts lower default; medical, small-business, renting and five-year loans predict higher.

Same risk — does a fintech loan or a bank loan default more?
Fintech loan
Bank loan
Example Identical FICO and income — one borrows on a marketplace platform to consolidate debt, one from a bank.
Result The fintech loan — significantly more, for equal risk. After consolidating, online borrowers tend to re-lever, spend more, and end up worse off.
