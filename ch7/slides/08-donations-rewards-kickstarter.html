Community-driven, not return-driven

Donations & rewards.

Donation-based · $7.0B (2020). No benefit promised — donors give to help. Charities, medical bills, civic projects, disaster relief. GoFundMe leads (acquired Crowdrise, 2017); volumes more than doubled during COVID-19.
Rewards-based · $1.3B (2020). Backers pre-purchase a future product. Kickstarter and Indiegogo dominate. Mandatory risk disclosure (2012) reduced backing of high-risk projects.
