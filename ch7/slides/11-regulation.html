A patchwork of rules. Some countries fold portals into existing bank/securities law (Germany, Singapore); others wrote new fintech-credit rules (UK, France, Switzerland). In Canada, online loans are securities — prospectus costs effectively exclude retail investors. In the US, funding portals must register with the SEC and join FINRA, and cannot give advice or hold client funds without a broker-dealer license.
The fintech charter. After years of legal challenges, the OCC granted its first special-purpose national bank charter to SoFi in January 2022 — bringing it “inside the federal bank regulatory perimeter… subject to the full panoply of bank regulations.”
