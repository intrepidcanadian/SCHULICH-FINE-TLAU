Benefits accrue to borrowers

Ease, speed, access.

Banks won’t lend to a start-up without revenue or collateral, and VCs wait for traction. Crowdfunding fills the pre-seed gap — or lets entrepreneurs pre-sell the product itself.

Why SMEs choose online lenders · US Fed survey
63% · speed of decision or funding
61% · chance of being funded
45% · no collateral required
13% · cost or interest rate (least cited)
32% of SMEs seeking funding applied to an online lender in 2018 (up from 19% in 2016) — despite lower satisfaction than with banks.
