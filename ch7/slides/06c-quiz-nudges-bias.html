Pop quiz · nudges & who succeeds.

Texts that remind borrowers of the consequences of not repaying — do they help or backfire?
Help repayment
Backfire
Example Text A: “We believe you’ll repay on time.”  Text B: “Miss a payment and your credit score will suffer.” (illustrative)
Result Backfire. Messages expressing positive expectations (Text A) raised repayment; reminders of negative consequences (Text B) had the opposite effect.

On Kickstarter, do women have higher or lower success rates than men?
Higher
Lower
Example Women run only ~35% of Kickstarter campaigns — so you might expect them to fare worse. (set-up)
Result Higher. Although women are just 35% of campaigns and set similar goals, they enjoy higher funding-success rates than men.
