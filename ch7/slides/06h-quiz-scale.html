Why do small businesses pick online lenders — mainly for low cost or for speed?
Low cost
Speed & access
Example US Fed survey — firms ranked speed of decision (63%), chance of being funded (61%), no collateral (45%)… and cost?
Result Speed & access. Cost / interest rate was the least-cited reason — just 13% — even though online loans are often more expensive.
Fintech Explained, Ch. 7 · US Fed Small Business Credit Survey, 2019 (note 34)
Which raises more money globally — debt or equity crowdfunding?
Debt
Equity
Example Equity platforms get the headlines (Wefunder, StartEngine, AngelList) — so you might assume they dominate the dollars.
Result Debt — by a mile. P2P + balance sheet lending is ~89–90% of all dollars raised; equity is just ~4%; donations and rewards make up the rest.
Fintech Explained, Ch. 7 · Table 7.1, CCAF (2020)
