Price discovery

Bookbuilding — the underwriter fixes one offer price (often mispriced).
Who can participate

Allocations to institutions and favored clients; retail buys after the pop.
Intermediary
None — a smart-contract pool is the venue.
An investment-bank underwriter and syndicate.
Cost of capital
Pool creation + gas + ~0.3% swap fees; no underwriting spread.
~7% underwriting fee plus legal, audit and listing costs.
Time to market
Live in minutes — deploy a pool.
6–18 months: S-1, SEC review, roadshow.
Lock-ups
None, or programmable vesting written into the contract.
90–180-day insider lock-ups.
Liquidity
Continuous 24/7 secondary trading from block one.
Liquidity begins at listing; market hours; T+2 settlement.
Disclosure & regulation
Minimal — no prospectus; a regulatory grey zone.
Prospectus, audited financials, ongoing reporting under securities law.
Allocation fairness
Open market — no favored allocations, but MEV / sniping / front-running risk.
Discretionary allocation; the first-day “pop” captured by insiders.
Investor protection
Caveat emptor — smart-contract and rug-pull risk, little recourse.
Underwriter due diligence, liability, and investor-protection rules.
