AAVE · DeFi lending protocol
Traditional bank
Interest rates
Algorithmic — set by a utilization curve, identical for everyone in the pool, updates every block.
Relationship-based — set by a credit committee, negotiated per borrower, sticky.
Collateral
Over-collateralized — post crypto worth more than you borrow (e.g. 70–80% LTV).
Under-collateralized or unsecured — lent on credit history and income.
Underwriting & KYC
None — permissionless, pseudonymous wallet, no credit check.
Mandatory KYC/AML, credit score, income and identity verification.
Loan term
Perpetual — no maturity; repay anytime; open until repaid or liquidated.
Fixed term and amortization schedule with a maturity date.
Custody
Non-custodial — a smart contract holds funds; you keep your keys.
Custodial — the bank holds your deposit; you’re an unsecured creditor.
Access & liquidity
Supply or withdraw instantly, 24/7 (subject to pool liquidity).
Business hours, settlement delays, withdrawal limits and notice periods.
Transparency
Every loan, collateral, rate and reserve is on-chain and auditable live.
Opaque balance sheet; periodic, aggregated disclosures.
Liquidation
Automatic — when health factor < 1, liquidators repay debt for collateral + bonus.
Manual collections, default workouts, courts — months to resolve.
Where the spread goes
Borrow–supply spread + reserve factor to the DAO treasury; no shareholder margin.
Net interest margin and fees accrue to shareholders.
Governance & safety net
AAVE holders vote on-chain; no deposit insurance (smart-contract/oracle risk).
Board, management & regulators; deposits FDIC/CDIC-insured to a limit.
