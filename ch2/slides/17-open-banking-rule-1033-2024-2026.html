§2.9b · data portability becomes law

Open Banking arrives in the US: CFPB Rule 1033, 2024→2026.

Slide 03 framed information asymmetry as one of the five intermediation dimensions; Rule 1033 attacks it head-on. On Oct 22, 2024 the CFPB finalized Rule 1033 (“Personal Financial Data Rights”), making consumer access to financial data — deposit, credit-card, payments — a federally enforceable right via authorised third-party APIs. Compliance phases in from Apr 2026 (largest banks) through 2030 (smallest). Litigation and a 2025 administration change have since reopened the rule for amendment, but the data-access architecture is now the regulatory baseline.
