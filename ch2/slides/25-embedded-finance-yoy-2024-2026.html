Embedded finance, year-over-year: $108.6B (2024) → $148B (2025) → $173B (2026).

Slide 12 framed embedded finance as a 2025 snapshot. Pull the camera back: 2024 baseline was $108.6B, 2025 closed at $148B, and 2026 trackers (Mordor / Marketintelo) put global revenue between $156B and $173B — a 26–31% YoY blended CAGR. The mix is shifting too: BaaS captured 34.2% of infrastructure spend in 2025, North America held 38.5% regional share, and merchants/SaaS platforms (not banks) are now the primary distribution layer.
