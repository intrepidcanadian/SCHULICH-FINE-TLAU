§2.10b · AI agents hit the quality wall

Klarna’s round trip: 700-FTE AI lift, then a hybrid retreat (2023-2026).

Slide 10 framed AI agents as the next monetization layer; the Klarna case is the cleanest economic test in fintech. In Feb 2024 Klarna’s OpenAI-built assistant handled 2.3M chats in one month — equivalent to 700 FTEs. Cost-per-transaction fell from $0.32 (Q1 2023) to $0.19 (Q1 2025). But by mid-2025 the CEO admitted full replacement had “gone too far”: customer satisfaction degraded on edge cases, escalations, and emotion. Klarna re-hired humans through 2025-26 and now runs a hybrid model. The unit economics are real; the quality ceiling is also real.
