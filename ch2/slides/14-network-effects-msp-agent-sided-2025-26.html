§2.12 · multi-sided platforms get a third side

Two-sided MSPs → agent-sided commerce.

Slide 08 framed Visa/PayPal as classic two-sided platforms (cardholders ↔ merchants). In Apr 2025 Visa launched Intelligent Commerce and Mastercard launched Agent Pay — opening their rails to a third side: autonomous AI agents acting on a consumer’s behalf. By Dec 2025 hundreds of agent-initiated transactions had cleared in production; Visa now forecasts mainstream adoption by Q4 2026.
