§2.11 · ten years after Philippon

Has fintech finally cracked the 2% puzzle?

Slide 04 cited Philippon's 2015 finding: ~2% intermediation cost, unchanged for 130 years. New BIS, ECB, and FDIC analyses (2024–25) show distributional compression in some segments — remittance, FX, retail brokerage — but the aggregate cost still hovers near 1.6–1.9% as compute savings get captured by intermediaries, not consumers.
