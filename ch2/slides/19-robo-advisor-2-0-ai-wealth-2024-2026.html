§2.7c · from automated allocation to AI-personalised wealth

Robo-advisors 2.0: AUM, scale, and AI personalisation 2024 → 2026.

Slide 05 cited Vanguard’s 2015 robo-advisor — an incumbent counter-attack. A decade later the “robo” layer is no longer a low-fee allocator. Wealthfront crossed $90B AUM and filed for IPO under WLTH (Oct 2025). Betterment crossed $65B AUM and 1M customers, acquired Ellevest (Feb 2025) and Rowboat Advisors (May 2025) for direct indexing. The wedge is no longer 0.25% pricing — it is AI-driven tax-loss harvesting at the individual-stock level, goal-based personalisation, and CFP-on-demand.
