§2.6b · same-side network effects in P2P

Network effects, observed: P2P payments 2020 → 2025.

Slide 08 used Venmo to illustrate same-side network effects ("payment utility rises with peer adoption"). Five years later, the bank-owned Zelle has decisively won the volume war — $1.2T in 2025, vs. $340B Venmo and ~$300B Cash App. Direct effects compound, but distribution (your bank vs. your peer group) shapes which loop wins.
