Embedded finance: from feature to business model.

Christensen’s “jobs to be done” framing sharpens in 2024–26: financial services are no longer destinations, they are SDK calls inside vertical SaaS, marketplaces, and AI agents. Stripe alone moved 1.3% of global GDP in 2024.
