AI agents: a new disruption vector.

Christensen’s framework applied to 2024–2026: agentic AI is targeting the most overserved tier of finance — mid-market wealth management, customer support, and back-office ops — with a 10× cost structure incumbents cannot match without dismantling their own labor model.

Step 01 · Overshoot
Premium advice
Wealth managers serve $1M+ AUM clients with $10K+ annual fees; mass-affluent under-served.
Step 02 · Low-end entry
AI advisor copilots
Morgan Stanley + OpenAI (2024) automated 80% of analyst-prep work for FAs; cost per query <$0.05.
Step 03 · Move upmarket
Agentic ops
Klarna AI agent (2024): handled 2.3M chats, work of 700 FTEs, $40M annual saving. SoFi, Brex, Mercury followed.
Step 04 · Displace
Autonomous finance
2025–26: agent-to-agent payments (Stripe Agents, Visa Intelligent Commerce). New value chain emerges.
