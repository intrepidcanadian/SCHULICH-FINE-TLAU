§3.11 · Y Combinator 2014 → 2025

From Wealthsimple to AI fintech-as-a-service.

Slides 04–05 anchor the 2014 era: Wealthsimple seed-pitched to 14 angels; YC reported “3,500+ companies funded.” By the W25 + S25 batches, YC funds twice as many fintech-adjacent startups per year, half are AI-native, and median check-size has compressed back to seed levels even as outcomes are larger and faster.
