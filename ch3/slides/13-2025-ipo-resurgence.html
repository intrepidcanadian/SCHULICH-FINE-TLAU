§3.7b · fintech IPO resurgence 2025

The 2025 IPO window: opened, then disciplined.

After a three-year drought, fintech IPOs returned in 2025 — but at sober prices. Klarna re-listed at one-third of its 2021 peak; Chime priced below private-market last rounds. The lesson: down-round IPOs can still clear when listings reset expectations.
