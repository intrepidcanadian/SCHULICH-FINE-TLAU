Class of 2025 IPOs: $3.2B raised, then the public-market reality test (Q1 2026).

Slide 13 caught the IPO window opening. This is the second beat: how Chime, Klarna, Circle & eToro have actually traded. Combined IPO proceeds were $3.2B (Invezz Dec 2025). Q1 2026 sorted them: Chime delivered its first profitable public quarter; Circle's USDC AUM compounded under GENIUS; Klarna ate margin from rising delinquencies; eToro held steady. The lesson Schulich students need: the IPO is the easy day — the next four earnings prints decide whether the multiple sticks.
