§3.7c · emerging-market fintech compounding

Nubank: from credit-card-only neobank to ~120M Latam customers, 2014→2025.

The Stripe and Wealthsimple arcs (slides 16, 17a) showed two North-American paths. Nubank is the largest fintech IPO of the post-2020 era and the clearest emerging-market test of the model. From 12M customers in 2019 it now serves ~120M across Brazil, Mexico, and Colombia — ~58% of Brazil’s adult population — while compounding revenue at >30% YoY and posting consistent GAAP profits. Market cap recovered from a ~$11B trough (mid-2022) to ~$70B+ by 2025.
