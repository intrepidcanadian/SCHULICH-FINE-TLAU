§3.7d · the AI-native funding bid

Capital concentrated into AI-native fintechs, 2023→2025.

Slide 11 framed the 2024-26 funding rebound. Inside that rebound, capital is concentrating: SMB-finance & corporate-card platforms that wrap LLM agents around the workflow now command 2−3× the multiples of vintage neobanks. Ramp went from $5.8B (Aug 2023) to $32B (Nov 2025) — a ~5.5× private mark in 27 months — while Brex’s mark slid from a $12.3B 2022 peak as it pivoted upmarket.
