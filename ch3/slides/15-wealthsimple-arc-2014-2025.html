§3.12 · the case study, ten years on

Wealthsimple AUA: $0 in 2014 → C$100B in 2025.

Slide 05’s case anchored Wealthsimple at its 2014 angel round. By Oct 2025 the firm crossed C$100B in assets under administration — three years ahead of its own plan — and closed a C$750M Series F at a C$10B post-money valuation. AUA doubled in twelve months. Of the 14 angels who said “yes” in 2014, the early backers now hold positions worth orders of magnitude more than their cheques.
