Plaid: $13.4B → $6.1B → ~$8–10B IPO. The whole 2021-2026 cycle in one company.

Slide 02 named Plaid as a Visa acquisition target ($5.3B blocked Nov 2020). Slide 13 listed Klarna, Chime and Circle reopening the IPO window. Plaid is the missing link — it shows what happens to a private fintech that doesn’t rush. April 2025: $575M secondary, valued at $6.1B (down 54% from the 2021 peak). FY2025 ARR: $546M, +40% YoY. Plaid told TechCrunch it would not IPO in 2025; analysts now project a 2026 listing at $8.5–10B. The arc is the cycle: 2021 mega-mark, 2022–2024 freeze, 2025 down round + profitability discipline, 2026 IPO at a re-earned mark.
