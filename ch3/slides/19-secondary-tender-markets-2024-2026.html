§3.13 · the IPO substitute

Tender offers replaced the IPO as the liquidity venue (2024-2026).

Slides 8 (IPO Performance) and 12 (IPO Window) framed the public exit. The bigger 2024-2026 story is companies staying private longer and using employer-led tender offers as a structured liquidity venue. Stripe alone re-rated from $50B (Mar 2023) to $159B (Feb 2026) entirely through tenders. Industry-wide secondary volume hit $240B in 2025, up from $162B in 2024 (Industry Ventures + Goldman Sachs estimates). The cap table arithmetic of slide 19 still holds — but the “exit” column now includes a private liquidity event that can recur every 6–12 months.
