Case study · Wealthsimple

From $2M seed to Canada’s largest robo-advisor.

Mike Katchen pitched a simpler wealth management platform for millennials at 35–50 bps. He raised $2M in a seed round from 14 angel investors in 2014. By 2021, Wealthsimple managed $15B+ AUM at a $5B valuation.
