§3.13 · the canonical fintech valuation arc

Stripe: $1.75B (2014) → $159B (2026) — through a $50B detour.

The closing slide cited Stripe at “$95B” — the 2021 mania peak. The company wrote that mark down 47% to $50B in 2023’s primary capital raise (Series I, $6.5B), climbed back to $91.5B by Feb 2025 on the back of stablecoin acquisitions (Bridge, $1.1B) and AI-merchant primitives, and crossed $159B in the Feb 2026 tender. Same enterprise; private mark moved $50B → $159B in three years. The cycle inside Ch. 3’s S-curve, made literal.
