ESG · the fastest-growing trend in asset management

Investing to tackle climate change.

Sustainable investing integrates environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors into asset selection. By 2020 it reached $35.3T — over a third of global AUM and up 54% since 2014. BlackRock found 54% of investors call it fundamental; they plan to double sustainable AUM from 18% to 37% by 2025.

Clim8 Invest · UK, 2019. A mobile app to invest in clean energy, clean tech, sustainable food, water, and recycling. From £25, three risk profiles, 0.60% fee — £12M raised and 42 staff by early 2022.
Case · Robinhood · Figure 8.3

Robinhood (2014) launched zero-commission trading to democratize finance — 75% of its first 150K users were born after 1980 — and forced the industry to follow. Its real revenue was payment for order flow: selling retail trade data to hedge funds.
