Financial services like tap water — just there when you need it

Beyond payments, one void at a time.

Users held large idle balances; merchants couldn’t get loans; consumers had no credit scores. Alipay built a product for each gap.

Money market fund · Yu’ebao · 2013
$250Bpeak, Mar 2018
“Spare treasure.” Paid 5–6% — double a bank deposit. At $165B in mid-2017 it was the world’s largest money market fund, ahead of JPMorgan.
Small-business lending · Aliloan · 2010
$5Bby 2017
Unsecured microloans with credit limits set by big-data analysis of merchant behavior. $2.1B disclosed at the 2014 IPO.
Credit scoring · Zhima (Sesame) · 2015
Big data
Consumer scores from payment history, online behavior, and social connections — where China had no credit-history system.
Digital bank · MYbank · 2015
5Mborrowers
Serving small businesses and rural farmers. ~RMB 30,000 average microloan; 2–4% non-performing. Ant holds a 30% stake. Plus wealth (Zhao Cai Bao).
