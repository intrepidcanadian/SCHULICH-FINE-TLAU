“Small and beautiful changes” · financial inclusion

Ant Group: a full-service intermediary.

Spun out ahead of the 2014 IPO, Ant Financial added consumer lending (Hua Bei), wealth (Ant Fortune), insurance (Ant Insurance, 80+ partners), equity crowdfunding (ANTSDAQ), and global payments — managing customers’ “FinLives.”

The IPO that wasn’t. Set to list at end-2020, the deal was pulled days before launch after Jack Ma criticized Chinese regulators. The banking regulator accused Ant of regulatory arbitrage. As of May 2022 the IPO remained shelved.
