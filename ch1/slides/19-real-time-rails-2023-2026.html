§3.6c · instant payments go mainstream

From batch to instant: real-time rails became table-stakes, 2023→2026.

Slide 10 listed instant settlement among the ten technologies. Three years on, every major payment market has a domestic real-time rail at scale. FedNow went from 35 launch banks (Jul 2023) to 1,400+ participants (Sep 2025); Brazil’s Pix processed 64B+ transactions in 2024; India’s UPI clears 17B+ transactions per month. Bank rails are no longer the bottleneck — the question is what gets built on top.
