§3.6d · the bitcoin wrapper goes mainstream

Spot crypto ETFs: from approval (Jan 2024) to $200B AUM (May 2026).

Slide 10 listed digital assets among the ten technologies. Two years after SEC approval (Jan 10, 2024), the spot bitcoin ETF complex crossed ~$200B AUM — faster than any prior ETF category. BlackRock’s IBIT alone holds ~810,000 BTC (~$66B). The wrapper, not the token, became the institutional access point: 401(k)s, pensions, and RIAs now buy bitcoin without custody. The Ether spot ETF (Jul 2024) followed; both reshape what “crypto in the portfolio” means for advisors.
