Stablecoins eat the SWIFT margin: B2B cross-border 2023 → 2026.

Slide 12 cited real-time rails. Slide 13 noted stablecoins crossed Visa+Mastercard combined card volume. The next layer down is B2B settlement — where ~92% of the global flow still runs through SWIFT correspondent banking. Stablecoin B2B payments grew from <$100M/month in early 2023 to >$6B/month by mid-2025 (a 733% YoY surge). Real-payment stablecoin volume doubled to ~$400B in 2025, ~60% of which is B2B. The card networks responded: Visa reported $4.6B annualized stablecoin settlement on its own rail in Q1 2026, and Wise Platform plugged 60+ banks (Bank Mandiri, IndusInd, Monzo, Brex) into a 20-second-or-less corridor.
