fourth-wave indicators

The 2024–2026 wave: AI, tokenization, real-time rails.

Fintech 3.0 is being eclipsed by a fourth wave defined by generative AI in the back office, on-chain settlement of regulated assets, and the universal arrival of instant payments.

01 · GenAI in Banking
$85B
Forecast global bank GenAI spend by 2027 (IDC, Aug 2024). JPMorgan deployed LLM Suite to 60K employees in 2024; Morgan Stanley rolled out GPT-4 advisor copilot to all FAs.
02 · Tokenization
$2.4B
BlackRock BUIDL tokenized treasury fund AUM (Q1 2025), launched March 2024 on Ethereum. Larry Fink: "the next generation for markets is tokenization of every asset."
03 · Real-Time Rails
$853B
FedNow 2025 transaction value (Federal Reserve), 8.4M settled payments, 1,400+ banks live. UK FPS, India UPI ($25T/yr), Brazil Pix all crossed local card volumes.
