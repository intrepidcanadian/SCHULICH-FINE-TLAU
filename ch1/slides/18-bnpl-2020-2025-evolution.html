§3.6b · BNPL goes mainstream & consolidates

BNPL: from pandemic novelty to public-market category, 2020→2025.

Slide 08 listed online lending alongside crowdfunding. Five years on, Buy-Now-Pay-Later is the breakout sub-category: 380M+ global users, $560B GMV in 2025, and the largest IPO of 2025. But the field also narrowed: Apple killed its in-house Pay Later, the CFPB’s 2024 interpretive rule was withdrawn in Apr 2025, and Affirm became the de-facto default checkout install for Apple Pay.
