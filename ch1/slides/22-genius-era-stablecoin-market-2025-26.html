§3.7f · post-GENIUS stablecoin landscape

The GENIUS Era: stablecoins move from gray zone to chartered rail (Jul 2025 → Q2 2026).

President Trump signed the GENIUS Act into law on 18 July 2025 — the first U.S. federal stablecoin framework. Title II created a payment-stablecoin issuer charter (state OR federal trust); Title VII banned issuer-paid interest. Nine months in, the market has compounded, not shrunk: total supply broke $280B in early 2026, USDC outpaced USDT growth for a second straight year, and Visa/Mastercard re-anchored to compliant USD-tokens. The thesis from slide 12 (the “fourth wave”) is now codified.
