§3.6f · post-GENIUS stablecoin landscape

The GENIUS Era: stablecoins move from gray zone to chartered rail (Jul 2025 → Q2 2026).

Trump signed the GENIUS Act on 18 July 2025 — the first U.S. federal stablecoin framework. The Act creates a federal payment-stablecoin issuer charter and prohibits issuer-paid interest to holders. Nine months in, total supply broke $280B in early 2026 (up from ~$250B at GENIUS signing), USDC outpaced USDT for a second straight year, and Visa/Mastercard re-anchored to compliant USD-tokens. The fourth-wave thesis is now codified.
