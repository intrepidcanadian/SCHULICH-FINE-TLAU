Putting the rails together — from your tap down to the central-bank ledger

How the pieces fit: message, clearing, settlement.

The takeaway: the message layers (Interac, SWIFT) carry instructions and hold no ledger; clearing (ACH) and settlement (Lynx) both bottom out on the central bank’s books — Lynx per payment in real time, ACH as a deferred net.
