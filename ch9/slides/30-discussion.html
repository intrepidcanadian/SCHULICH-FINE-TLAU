Take it to the seminar — payments first, then insurance

Questions for discussion.

Payments, money transfer & FX
  1. What are the main pain points facing customers in payments, money transfer, and foreign exchange?
  2. How important are payments for banks, and what are the different sources of revenue?
  3. What are the payment rails, and how does the large value transfer system differ from the small-payments system?
  4. Who are the players in the payments ecosystem, and what role does each play?
  5. What are the sources of revenue for payment companies — and which are fee-based versus risk-based?
  6. What has been the strategy for new entrants, and how did they benefit from technological and regulatory change?
  7. What are examples of payment pain points and the fintechs that solved them?
Insurance & insurtech
  1. What are the different segments of the insurance market, and how can we assess their relative size?
  2. What are insurtechs, and what has been their experience disrupting the insurance industry?
  3. What are examples of pain points solved by insurtechs — in customer-facing and back-office businesses?
The throughline. Across both industries, the winning move is the same — solve a sharp customer pain point, then automate the processing behind it. Keep that lens on every example above.
