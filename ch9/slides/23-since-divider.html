Since the textbook: 2022–2025.

The chapter’s data effectively freezes around 2022. Four shifts have reshaped these profit pools since — real-time rails went mainstream, stablecoins gained legal footing, the insurtech boom turned to winter and then to AI, and regulators finally caught up.

01
Real-time rails
Instant account-to-account payments go mainstream — FedNow, UPI, Pix, EU mandate.
02
Stablecoins
A credible new cross-border layer — and the first US federal law to govern it.
03
Winter → AI
Insurtech funding collapses ~76%; survivors reach profit; capital rotates to AI.
04
Agentic & AI-native
AI agents make payments; GenAI runs underwriting and claims; CBDCs diverge.
