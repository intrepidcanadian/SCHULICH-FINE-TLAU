Paytm · “pay through mobile” · founded 2010 by Vijay Shekhar Sharma · New Delhi

India’s largest payments platform — still deep in losses.

From phone top-ups to a super-app: 337M consumers and 22M merchants by its 2021 IPO — India’s largest ever, valuing it at $18.5B. The stock fell 27% on day one and is down 66% since.
