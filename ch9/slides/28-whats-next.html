Where payments and insurance are heading next

Agents pay, AI underwrites, money goes programmable.

Four fronts to watch
agentic commerce
AI agents pay
Stripe + OpenAI “Instant Checkout” and the Agentic Commerce Protocol (Sep 2025); Google’s AP2 with 60+ partners; Visa Intelligent Commerce & Mastercard Agent Pay (Apr 2025); PayPal agentic checkout (Oct 2025).
AI-native ops
GenAI runs the back office
Insurer GenAI adoption ~doubled in 2025 (~55% deploying). Lemonade reports AI handling 96% of first loss notices and resolving 55% of claims end-to-end — the automation thesis, realized.
BNPL matures
Public & profitable
Klarna IPO’d on the NYSE (Sep 2025, ~$15B — far below its 2021 peak); Affirm posted its first profitable fiscal year (FY25). Regulators are now formalizing BNPL rules.
CBDCs diverge
Programmable money splits
China’s e-CNY is the largest live CBDC (~$2.3T cumulative); the digital euro advances toward ~2029; the US banned a CBDC (executive order 2025, later codified).
The throughline still holds. Every front above is the same playbook the chapter described — solve a sharp pain point, then automate the processing — now supercharged by AI and programmable money. The winners will still be decided by the business model, not the technology.
