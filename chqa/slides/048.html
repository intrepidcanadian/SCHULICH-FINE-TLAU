Chapter 9 · Payments & Insurance

How important are payments for banks and what are the different sources of revenue?

Payments are highly important, generating close to 40% of bank revenues from fees and interest charges, with global revenues of US$1.9 trillion in 2020.
—North America payments earned $485 billion in 2020, 44% from credit card interest and fees.
—28% domestic payment fees, 20% account/overdraft interest, 9% cross-border fees.
—JPMorgan: 47 million Chase customers made 69 fee-generating transactions monthly.
—65% of clients would leave a firm lacking integrated digital channels.
