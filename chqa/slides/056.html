Chapter 9 · Payments & Insurance

What are some examples of pain points solved by different insurtechs in customer-facing and back-office businesses?

Customer-facing insurtechs personalize products and pricing while back-office insurtechs automate claims and administration to make insurance cheaper, faster, and more convenient.
—Root customizes auto insurance using smartphone sensors to price driving behavior.
—Lemonade uses AI bots Maya and Jim to sell policies and pay claims in seconds.
—Cyence modeled cyber risk, acquired by Guidewire for $275 million in 2017.
—Snapsheet processes auto claims by photo, estimating repairs in 2.7 hours on average.
