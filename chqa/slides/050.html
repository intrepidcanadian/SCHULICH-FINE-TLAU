Chapter 9 · Payments & Insurance

Who are the players in the payments ecosystem and what roles do they play?

Players include the customer, issuing bank, credit card network, merchant bank (acquirer), payment gateway, and payment processor, plus banks and money service businesses.
—Issuing bank approves the card, lends funds, and bills the customer.
—Card networks (VISA, MasterCard, Amex) set rules and interchange fees.
—Merchant bank/acquirer provides the POS machine and receives merchant funds.
—Payment gateway securely transmits data; processor routes messages between gateway and banks.
