Chapter 11 · Techfins & Bigtech

How serious is the threat from techfins and bigtech for incumbent financial institutions? Will banks, insurance companies, and asset managers become obsolete?

The answer is no; the entry of techfins and bigtech into financial services has had mixed success and will not make incumbents obsolete.
—Chinese techfins succeeded by addressing institutional voids but always worked with domestic banks.
—North American bigtech built payment footholds but largely failed to move beyond payments.
—Competition has forced banks to become more customer-centric and innovative.
—The winning combination will be bigtech-bank partnerships that combine their respective strengths.
