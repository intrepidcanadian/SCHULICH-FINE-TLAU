Chapter 10 · Digital Banking

What is banking-as-a-service (BaaS) and what are the advantages for both the fintech and the bank partner?

BaaS lets a licensed bank provide the regulated back office so fintechs offer white-labeled banking products, letting each side focus on its strengths.
—The bank holds deposits, underwrites loans, and satisfies capital, KYC, and AML requirements.
—The fintech looks and acts like a bank while focusing on the customer technology experience.
—Smaller regional banks like WebBank, Bancorp, and Cross River seized this opportunity over giants.
—Customers gain FDIC-insured deposits and global payments; WebBank served Klarna and LendingClub.
