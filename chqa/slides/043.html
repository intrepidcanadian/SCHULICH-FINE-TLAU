Chapter 8 · Digital Wealth

What is meant by digital wealth management? What technologies are being used to transform this industry and how?

Digital wealth management leverages technology to automate and improve the customer experience while generating fees from asset acquisition, portfolio management, and back-office automation.
—Uses cloud computing, big data, APIs, AI, blockchain, and cryptography.
—Automates repetitive tasks to lower costs, raise productivity, and add investor value.
—Addresses use-cases like CRM, digital identity, cybersecurity, and regtech compliance.
—Brings HNW-level personalized services (tax-loss harvesting, smart beta) to retail at low cost.
