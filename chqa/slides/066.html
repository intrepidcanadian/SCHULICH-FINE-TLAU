Chapter 10 · Digital Banking

What are some of the strategies adopted by incumbents in response to fintech new entrants?

Incumbents choose from a menu of options depending on capabilities and scale: build in-house, R&D labs, partner, license, invest, acquire, or target fintechs as customers.
—Bigger players build apps in-house (Goldman's Marcus) or set up innovation labs, risking innovation theater.
—Partnerships include Canada's Interac e-Transfers and the US banks' Zelle joint venture against Venmo.
—Banks license fintech software (JPMorgan with Plaid) or buy equity via corporate VC arms like RBC Ventures.
—Acquisitions like JPMorgan-WePay can succeed while BPCE-Fidor failed from culture clash.
