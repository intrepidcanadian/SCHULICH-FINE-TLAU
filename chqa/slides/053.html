Chapter 9 · Payments & Insurance

What are examples of pain points in payments and the fintechs that solved them?

Fintechs solved pain points in e-commerce trust, money transfers, foreign exchange, remittances, and cashless payment, including Alipay, M-PESA, Wise, WorldRemit, and Octopus.
—Alipay created trust in e-commerce via escrow accounts releasing funds only on delivery.
—M-PESA democratized money transfers for unbanked Kenyans using mobile phones.
—Wise lowered FX costs with mid-market rates and transparent flat fees.
—WorldRemit cut remittance costs to a $3.99 fee and 1% conversion rate.
