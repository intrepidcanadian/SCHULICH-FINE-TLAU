Chapter 10 · Digital Banking

How are challenger banks doing around the world and who are some leading players?

Challenger banks are thriving, with over 300 globally and fastest growth in Brazil, India, and the US; leaders include Revolut, N26, Nubank, and Chime.
—Four leading banks together served 111 million customers as of year-end 2022.
—COVID-19 accelerated adoption; app downloads worldwide exceeded 264 million, led by Latin America.
—Nubank had 75 million customers by 2022; Revolut was valued at US$33 billion.
—Growth was strongest in markets with many underbanked and unbanked consumers.
