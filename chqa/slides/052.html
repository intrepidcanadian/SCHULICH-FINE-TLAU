Chapter 9 · Payments & Insurance

What has been the strategy for new entrants into payments and how did they benefit from technological and regulatory changes?

New entrants started with a single use-case, built a foothold with core customers, then expanded the product offering to capture more of the value chain and reach mainstream customers.
—Single use-cases: mobile money, fair FX rates, or quick, inexpensive remittances.
—Benefited from internet, smartphones, 3G/4G/5G networks, and cloud computing.
—Benefited from real-time gross settlement, NFC, tap-and-go, QR codes, and blockchain.
—Regulatory changes promoting open banking, open APIs, and opening the payment rails.
