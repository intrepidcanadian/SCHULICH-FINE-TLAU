Chapter 7 · Crowdfunding & P2P

Why are peer-to-peer (P2P) platforms now called marketplace platforms?

The term changed because most funding now comes from institutional investors rather than individuals, making it no longer truly peer-to-peer.
—Individual capital was insufficient, so portals accepted hedge funds, pension funds, banks, and insurers.
—Around 85% of Prosper's funds come from institutional investors.
—LendingClub and Funding Circle shut their retail channels to work only with institutions.
