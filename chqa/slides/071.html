Chapter 11 · Techfins & Bigtech

How did the instant messaging and gaming company Tencent move into financial services? What was the first financial product that it launched?

Tencent moved into finance by adding a payment feature to its messaging platform, first launching the proprietary payment service TenPay in 2005 in response to Alipay.
—TenPay's uptake was slow until WeChat Pay went viral via the 2014 Red Envelope campaign.
—WeChat Pay reached nearly 40% of third-party mobile payments by year-end 2018.
—Diversified into a money market fund (Licaitong), challenger bank (WeBank), and insurtech (WeSure).
—By 2022, WeChat and WeChat Pay had 1.3 billion monthly active users.
