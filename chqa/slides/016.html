Chapter 6 · Ethereum & DeFi

What is ether (ETH) and what role does it play on the Ethereum network?

Ether (ETH) is Ethereum's native token, described by Buterin as the internal crypto fuel used to buy the 'gas' required to run dapps.
—The amount of gas depends on the computational resources a program requires.
—This system incentivizes developers to write efficient applications and deters malicious behavior.
—ETH was verified via proof-of-work until September 2022, then proof-of-stake.
—There is no cap on ETH; around 120 million were in circulation in April 2022.
