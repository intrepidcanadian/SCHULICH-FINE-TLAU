Chapter 11 · Techfins & Bigtech

What are the three competitive strengths of the techfins and bigtech in financial services?

The three competitive strengths are platform ecosystems and network effects, access to customers and brand recognition, and big data insights and customer experience.
—Platform ecosystems generate same-side and cross-side network effects that make platforms sticky.
—Massive loyal user bases and strong brands, while banks' branch networks and brand trust have eroded.
—Proprietary data analyzed with AI and machine learning to understand customer needs before they arise.
—The advantage is not the data itself but how it is used to understand a customer's needs.
