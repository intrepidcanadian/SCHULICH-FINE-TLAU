Chapter 5 · Bitcoin, Blockchain & Crypto

What is Bitcoin? How did its creator Satoshi Nakamoto address the double-spend problem?

Bitcoin is the first cryptocurrency, launched in 2009 by the anonymous Satoshi Nakamoto to create a peer-to-peer electronic cash system without a central authority.
—Double-spend problem: verifying money was not spent twice without a trusted intermediary.
—Satoshi used a time-stamped electronic ledger secured against alteration using cryptography.
—It relied on a P2P network of nodes where transactions were collected in blocks and hashed.
—Identical public copies of the ledger are distributed across the network, over 46,000 nodes in 2023.
