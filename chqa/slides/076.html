What was the experience of Google with its payment product? How did it evolve over time?

Google struggled with product-market fit, relaunching its payment product multiple times while losing ground to Apple Pay and Venmo.
—Started 2011 with Google Wallet, then Android Pay (2015) and Google Tez in India.
—In 2018 it merged Wallet and Android Pay into Google Pay.
—By late 2020 it had 150 million users versus Apple Pay's 500+ million.
—Abandoned Google Plex bank accounts in October 2021, shifting to B2B focus.
