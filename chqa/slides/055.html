Chapter 9 · Payments & Insurance

What are insurtechs and what has been their experience disrupting the insurance industry?

Insurtechs are fintechs exploiting mobile apps, cloud, biometrics, sensors, data science, and AI to disrupt insurance; their experience has been rapid growth mixed with many failures.
—Insurtechs raised close to $50 billion between 2018 and 2022, $17.8 billion in 2021.
—CB Insights tracked 44 insurtech unicorns globally in 2022.
—One-quarter of 760 sampled insurtechs shut down within 2019, often from lack of market appetite.
—IPO performance was mixed: Lemonade +55% and Duck Creek +13%, but GoHealth -48% and Root -88%.
