Chapter 6 · Ethereum & DeFi

What problems did Vitalik Buterin see with the Bitcoin network for the future of the internet of value (Web3)?

Buterin concluded Bitcoin was inefficient, lacked scalability, and had a limited scripting language, making it a faulty foundation for Web3.
—Bitcoin transactions were slow due to limited block size and time-consuming mining.
—The Bitcoin blockchain only recorded BTC and could not accommodate other cryptoassets.
—The Bitcoin scripting language was too simplistic for generalized applications.
—He proposed a superior foundational protocol allowing others to build decentralized applications on top.
