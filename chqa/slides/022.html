Chapter 6 · Ethereum & DeFi

What is total value locked and how it is measured?

Total value locked (TVL) is the dollar value of cryptocurrencies locked in smart contracts, liquidity pools, or staked on a PoS blockchain, and is the main measure of DeFi market size.
—TVL is the most widely used measure of the health of crypto.
—The DeFi market was close to $40 billion at year-end 2022, down from $175 billion.
—DeFi represented 5% of all cryptocurrencies' $800 billion market capitalization.
—Figures are tracked by the website DefiLlama, as no official statistics exist.
