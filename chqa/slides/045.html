Chapter 8 · Digital Wealth

What is a technology-enabled eAdvisor, and why is a hybrid model so important for the future of the wealth management industry?

An eAdvisor is a technology-enabled financial advisor who manages a larger client book using digital tools, and the hybrid model matters because human advisors will be supported, not replaced, by algorithms.
—Fidelity found eAdvisors have 42% higher median AUM and 35% more AUM per client.
—eAdvisors use video conferencing, e-signature, data aggregation, and client segmentation.
—Grace and Sarta and EY (Advisor 2.0) argue technology automates boring tasks and improves experience.
—Hybrid is vital because many investors, especially retirees and boomers, still want a human.
