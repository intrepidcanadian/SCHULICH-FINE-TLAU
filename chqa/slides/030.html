Chapter 7 · Crowdfunding & P2P

Who is borrowing from online lenders and who is providing funding? How do lenders assess the risk of default for online loans?

Borrowers are individuals and small businesses; funding comes from both retail and institutional investors; portals assess default risk via credit ratings reflected in interest rates.
—Individuals borrow up to $50,000 and small businesses up to $500,000, terms up to 5 years.
—Average loan was $8,500, 3.25-year maturity, borrower age 49 with a 654 credit score.
—Portals collect hard and soft data, assign credit ratings, and price loans by default risk.
—Institutional investors use APIs and robot algorithms to screen and outperform less sophisticated investors.
