Chapter 11 · Techfins & Bigtech

Why did Alibaba create Alipay and how did this business grow over time? How is Alipay related to Ant Financial, later known as Ant Group?

Alibaba created Alipay in 2004 to build trust between buyers and sellers by holding payments in escrow, filling institutional voids in China's underdeveloped payment system.
—Grew from 100 million users in 2008 to 300 million by March 2010; launched mobile payments in 2009.
—Expanded into money market funds, wealth management, lending, credit scoring, and banking.
—In 2014 Alibaba spun off financial units into Ant Financial Services ahead of its US IPO.
—Ant Financial was later renamed Ant Group, whose 2020 IPO was pulled by China's central bank.
