Chapter 6 · Ethereum & DeFi

What is decentralized finance (DeFi) and what are the main activities across different blockchains?

DeFi is a movement to build a global, decentralized financial system on cryptoassets, blockchains, and smart contracts, disintermediating traditional financial intermediaries.
—It creates a system that is open, permissionless, transparent, and automated.
—Biggest categories are DEXes, borrowing/lending, and liquidity staking.
—Ethereum dominates with close to 60% of TVL, followed by Tron (10.8%) and BSC (10.5%).
—Other activities include yield farming, bridge tokens, CDPs, and derivatives.
