Chapter 10 · Digital Banking

What is a challenger bank and how is it different from a traditional bricks-and-mortar bank?

A challenger bank (also neobank or digital bank) offers the same banking products as bricks-and-mortar banks but delivers an entirely electronic, end-to-end digital experience.
—Account opening takes under 10 minutes on mobile with no branch visit, though KYC ID upload is required.
—The bank is open 24/7, 365 days, with intuitive dashboards, gamification, and chatbots.
—Value proposition is lower cost, transparent fees, fast service, and better customer experience.
—Initially appealed to millennials and Gen Z who valued convenience and speed.
