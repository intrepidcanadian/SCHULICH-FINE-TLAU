What is a token and what are the different categories used by dapps?

A token is a digital asset whose ownership is recorded on a blockchain, categorized broadly as fungible (ERC-20) or non-fungible (ERC-721).
—Fungible tokens include payment, utility (service), security, and governance tokens.
—Payment tokens like ETH buy things; utility tokens like BAT claim a service.
—Governance tokens grant voting rights, such as the Maker token for DAI decisions.
—Non-fungible tokens (NFTs) represent unique, indivisible items like art or collectibles.
