Chapter 6 · Ethereum & DeFi

What is a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) and what happened to the one created by Slock.It?

A DAO is a virtual organization operating by rules coded in smart contracts; Slock.It's DAO was hacked and drained of ETH.
—Slock.It launched the first DAO on April 29, 2016, raising $150 million from 11,000 members.
—Hackers siphoned off $40 million of ETH on June 17, 2016.
—The response sparked a debate over blockchain immutability and a decisive vote.
—It culminated in a hard fork splitting Ethereum (ETH) and Ethereum Classic (ETC).
