Chapter 5 · Bitcoin, Blockchain & Crypto

What have researchers found explains price changes and the cross-section of returns in Bitcoin?

Researchers found cryptocurrency returns are driven by user adoption, investor attention, and momentum, while three factors explain the cross-section of returns across coins.
—Returns were driven by adoption metrics like wallet users, active addresses, transactions, and payments.
—Returns were unrelated to electricity or computing costs; Google Bitcoin searches and momentum predicted returns.
—The three cross-sectional factors are the crypto market, coin size, and price momentum.
—Small coins and low-price coins had higher average returns; the market tends to follow Bitcoin.
