Chapter 8 · Digital Wealth

What are examples of fintechs attacking different parts of the wealth management value chain?

Fintechs target specific value-chain pain points such as digital onboarding, portfolio management, sustainable investing, and commission-free trading.
—Nest Wealth pivoted to B2B, offering paperless onboarding to cut advisors' 15 hours/week of admin.
—BlackRock's Aladdin Wealth licensed to 16 firms, combining front-, middle-, back-office functions.
—Clim8 Invest lets UK retail investors put money into ESG/sustainable funds from 25 pounds.
—Robinhood launched zero-commission trading in 2014 to democratize retail investing.
