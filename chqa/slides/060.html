Chapter 10 · Digital Banking

What is the value proposition of a fintech app developer and their strategy for building and scaling their business?

Fintech app developers provide an innovative, inexpensive solution to a specific pain point, then unbundle a profitable bank product and later rebundle complementary services.
—Success requires simple onboarding, intuitive UX, strong security, and a personalized experience.
—Unbundling offers a better single product at lower cost, avoiding bank cross-subsidies and loss leaders.
—Establish a foothold with early adopters like millennials, then rebundle to make customers sticky.
—Fits Christensen's disruptive innovation: start cheaper and good enough, then move up-market.
