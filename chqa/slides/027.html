Chapter 7 · Crowdfunding & P2P

What are the key differences between rewards-based and donation-based crowdfunding?

Rewards-based crowdfunding pre-sells a future product to backers who receive a reward, while donation-based asks for contributions with no promised benefit.
—Donors give to help causes like charities, medical expenses, or disaster relief, not for returns.
—Backers fund new product/service development and are promised a reward on completion.
—Kickstarter and Indiegogo are rewards-based; GoFundMe is the largest US donation-based portal.
