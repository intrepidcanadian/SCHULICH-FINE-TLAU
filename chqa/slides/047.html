Chapter 9 · Payments & Insurance

What are some of the pain points facing customers in the payments, money transfer, and foreign exchange businesses?

Customers face payments that are slow, non-transparent, and costly, especially across borders, alongside poor customer service and many hidden fees from existing intermediaries.
—A bank wire costs around $35 and takes several business days, even domestically.
—Overseas remittances can cost around 10% and take weeks to arrive.
—FX exchanges routinely charge 3% or more, often hidden as 'zero commission'.
—Incumbents provide poor service and charge many fees with little transparency.
