Chapter 10 · Digital Banking

What is an example of a customer pain point in banking and how does a fintech address this?

A common pain point is consumers not knowing their credit score; fintechs like Credit Karma and Borrowell address it by giving away free credit scores and reports.
—Credit scores from Equifax and TransUnion measure default risk yet most consumers are unaware of theirs.
—Credit Karma's free app attracted 4 million users in four years and became profitable.
—In 2019 an estimated 7 million US households were unbanked, lacking checking or savings accounts.
—Fintechs deliver an experience that is easier, cheaper, faster, and more convenient.
