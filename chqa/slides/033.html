Chapter 7 · Crowdfunding & P2P

What has been the growth of alternative finance globally and where is the most capital raised? What has happened in China?

Alternative finance has raised close to $1.5 trillion over eight years at a 33% CAGR, now led by the US, after China's market collapsed amid fraud.
—In 2017 China raised over $350 billion (86%); by 2020 it was just $1.2 billion (1%).
—In 2020 the US led with $73.6 billion (65%), then Europe and the UK at $22.6 billion (20%).
—China saw widespread fraud, Ponzi schemes, and over 84% of platforms failing after a regulatory crackdown.
—Ezubao raised $8 billion then collapsed as a Ponzi scheme; over 900,000 investors lost about $7.6 billion.
