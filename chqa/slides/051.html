Chapter 9 · Payments & Insurance

What are the sources of revenue for payment and money transfer companies? Which revenues are fee-based and which are risk-based?

Payment players earn fee-based revenue from interchange, assessment, processing, and conversion fees, and risk-based income from taking FX, liquidity, and credit risk exposures.
—Fee-based: interchange (1.75%), assessment/swipe fee (0.11%), processing fee, cross-border conversion up to 3%.
—Risk-based: FX risk earns the bid-ask spread on currency inventories.
—Risk-based: liquidity risk from depositing collateral; credit risk from potential customer default.
—Banks also charge indirect fees: account, maintenance, NSF, and trade finance fees.
