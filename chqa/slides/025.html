Chapter 7 · Crowdfunding & P2P

What are the two types of online portals for alternative finance?

Online portals are divided into investment and non-investment crowdfunding, distinguished by what the contributor expects in return for their money.
—Investment portals issue debt (loans) or equity, where contributors expect a financial return.
—Non-investment portals are donation-based or rewards-based, driven by non-financial motivations.
—Debt crowdfunding dominates at roughly 89% of capital raised in 2020.
