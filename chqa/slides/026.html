Chapter 7 · Crowdfunding & P2P

What are the two types of investment crowdfunding and how are they different?

The two types are debt and equity: debt is a loan repaid with interest, while equity is an ownership stake in the business.
—Debt (P2P/marketplace or balance sheet lending) creates a contractual obligation to repay principal plus interest.
—Equity sells common shares, giving investors a legal claim on profits and assets.
—In 2020 debt raised $101 billion (89%) versus equity's $4.4 billion (4%).
