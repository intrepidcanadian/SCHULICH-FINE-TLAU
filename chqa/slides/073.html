Chapter 11 · Techfins & Bigtech

What has been Amazon's path into financial services? What financial products does it offer and why?

Amazon built a payments foundation starting in 2007 and expanded through trial-and-error into select financial products that support buyers and sellers on its e-commerce platform.
—Amazon Pay (2007) provides a digital wallet with no interchange fee.
—Amazon Lending (2011) offers merchant loans from $1,000 to $750,000 by invitation.
—Amazon Cash (2017) lets unbanked customers add cash deposits via CVS and 7-Eleven.
—It builds internally to increase marketplace sales, not to become a conventional bank.
