Chapter 10 · Digital Banking

Why do fintechs want to adopt a B2B model and white-label their mobile apps to incumbents?

B2C customer acquisition is very costly ($500 to $1,000 per customer), so many fintechs pivot to B2B, licensing white-labeled apps to banks that have large customer bases.
—Incumbents have deep pockets, regulatory expertise, and are perfect customers held back by legacy IT.
—White-label means the bank's brand replaces the fintech's, as with Sensibill and Moven.
—SaaS licensing brings stable recurring revenues attractive to shareholders and a path to exit.
—Banks gain innovative solutions without developing them in-house.
