Which DeFi activities do you find most interesting and why?

The chapter presents several notable DeFi activities including decentralized exchanges, collateralized debt positions, liquidity staking, and derivatives, each demonstrating the 'crypto LEGO' potential.
—DEXes like Curve use automated market makers and liquidity pools to swap tokens.
—MakerDAO acts as a decentralized central bank, minting stablecoin DAI against collateral.
—Liquidity staking via Lido lets users double-dip returns using stTokens.
—Synthetix derivatives give on-chain exposure to off-chain assets through 'synths'.
