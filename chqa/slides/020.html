Chapter 6 · Ethereum & DeFi

What is the purpose of a stablecoin and what are the different types? What distinguishes Tether from DAI?

A stablecoin is a payment token kept stable by pegging to another asset, solving cryptocurrency volatility; Tether is centralized while DAI is decentralized.
—Types are backed by fiat currency, cryptocurrencies, commodities, or nothing (algorithmic).
—Tether (USDT) is a centralized coin controlled by the founders of the Bitfinex exchange.
—DAI is a decentralized stablecoin controlled by the MakerDAO smart contract.
—Stablecoins are typically pegged at $1.00 and backed by collateral.
