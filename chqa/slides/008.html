Chapter 5 · Bitcoin, Blockchain & Crypto

What are altcoins and what were the first ones created? Were they mined or sold through an initial coin offering?

Altcoins are Alternatives to Bitcoin, early cryptocurrencies made by modifying Bitcoin's source code with different features and governance.
—The first altcoins were Litecoin and Namecoin in 2011, then Peercoin in 2012.
—In 2013 came Dogecoin, Gridcoin, Primecoin, Nxt, and Ripple XRP.
—Many competing cryptocurrencies were pre-mined and sold to the public through ICOs.
—Regulators effectively shut down the ICO market in mid-2018 due to widespread frauds.
