How has Apple found success in embedding financial services into its iOS ecosystem?

Apple succeeded by partnering with incumbents to embed banking into its ecosystem and protect its smartphone market share, using financial apps as loss leaders rather than profit sources.
—Apple Pay (2014) uses NFC, tokenization, and biometric security; reached 507 million users by 2020.
—Apple Card (2019), issued by MasterCard and backed by Goldman Sachs, has no fees and 2% cashback.
—Apple Card Family (2021) shares credit lines with 3% cashback.
—A savings account (2023) at 4.15% interest, backed by Goldman Sachs, was added.
