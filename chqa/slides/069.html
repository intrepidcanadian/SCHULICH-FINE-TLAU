Chapter 11 · Techfins & Bigtech

What is a techfin and why is their name the reverse of fintech?

A techfin is a technology platform (like Alibaba, Tencent) that offers financial services second, with the name reversing 'fintech' to signal it is a technology company first.
—Term coined by Alibaba's Jack Ma in 2016 to describe Alipay.
—They begin with e-commerce, social media, or gaming, then add finance.
—They expand from payments into money market funds, wealth management, and insurance.
—Financial services are enablers or loss leaders to attract and retain platform customers.
