Chapter 8 · Digital Wealth

What is the wealth management industry, how big is it, and who works in it?

The wealth management industry manages over $100 trillion of investments across financial planning, investing, and operations, employing a wide range of investment professionals.
—Robo-advice at ~$1.8 trillion is less than 2% of the $100 trillion industry.
—Job titles include financial advisor, planner, portfolio manager, trader, private banker, risk manager.
—Has a wholesale (institutional) side and a retail (individual investor) side.
—Advisors must be registered (Series 7, Canadian Securities Course) and may hold CFA or CFP designations.
