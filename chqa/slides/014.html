Chapter 6 · Ethereum & DeFi

What is the Ethereum network and who created it?

Ethereum is an open-source, programmable blockchain supporting smart contracts, dapps, and DAOs, created by Russian-Canadian programmer Vitalik Buterin.
—Buterin posted the Ethereum whitepaper in November 2013 at age 19.
—Its native currency ether (ETH) buys the 'gas' needed to run dapps.
—It is the foundation for decentralized finance (DeFi), a disruptive financial innovation.
—The Ethereum Foundation officially launched the blockchain on July 30, 2015.
