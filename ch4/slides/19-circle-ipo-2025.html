§4.7c · stablecoin issuer enters public markets

Circle IPO · the first big stablecoin float, June 2025.

The tokenized RWA slide (13) framed asset tokenization. The supply-side equivalent is the issuer balance sheet. On Jun 5, 2025 Circle Internet Group (NYSE: CRCL) priced at $31, raising ~$1.05B at a ~$6.9B fully-diluted valuation; the stock more than doubled on day 1 and traded above $200 within weeks, peaking at a ~$60B market cap. The model: USDC float earns short-rate yield; sharing economics with Coinbase + the GENIUS Act (US stablecoin law, signed Jul 18, 2025) reshape the multiple.
