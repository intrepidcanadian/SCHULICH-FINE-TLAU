Financial intermediaries · no DCF possible

Valuing banks: JPMorgan.

Banks lack sales and EBITDA — enterprise multiples and DCF cannot be used. Analysts rely on equity multiples (P/E, P/Book) and ratios (NIM, efficiency, ROA, ROE).
