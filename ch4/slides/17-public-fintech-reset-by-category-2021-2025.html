Public fintech reset by category — not all sectors recovered.

Slide 11’s multiples table averaged across categories. The averaging hides everything: payment networks (V/MA) re-rated to all-time highs, neobanks (NU) compounded, BNPL (AFRM) round-tripped, while crypto (COIN) and lending (SOFI) followed cycles. The right reference set still depends on which kind of fintech you’re valuing.
