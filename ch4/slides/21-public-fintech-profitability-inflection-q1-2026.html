Q1 2026 earnings: the “profitable fintech” archetype is now real.

Slide 11 priced fintech multiples on the assumption that Rule-of-40 candidates are scarce. They are not anymore. SoFi posted its 10th consecutive profitable quarter in Q1 2026 (net income $167M, +134% YoY) on $1.1B revenue (+41%). Robinhood netted $346M with 15% revenue growth. Both are now S&P-style operating-leverage stories, not narrative-only growth stocks. This re-rates the “public fintech” multiples lens: discount rate falls, terminal-value share rises, and the bank/neobank gap (slide 20) narrows from the neobank side.
