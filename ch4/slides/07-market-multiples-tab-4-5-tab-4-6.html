§4.5 · Relative valuation
07 / 23
Mature companies · comparable analysis

Market multiples & precedent transactions.

Relative valuation standardizes value via equity multiples (P/E) and enterprise multiples (EV/Sales, EV/EBITDA). Use 3–5 comparables; calculate avg/median, drop outliers. Precedent transactions add a control premium.

Tab. 4.5 · Common Market MultiplesSource: Fintech Explained
Three steps to relative valuation
1. Identify comparables (industry, geo, size) · 2. Choose multiples (LTM/NTM, equity/enterprise) · 3. Generate implied value (15× P/E × $10M = $150M)
Precedent: 3×, 2×, 4×, 5×, 8× EV/Sales. Avg = 4.4×; $50M target → $220M. Drop outlier → 3.5× = $175M (−20%).
