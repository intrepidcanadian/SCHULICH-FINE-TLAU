Fintech multiples: a wide spectrum.

NTM multiples and ratios for seven listed fintechs. Extreme variation highlights why relative valuation is unreliable for younger fintechs.

PayPal — most attractive?
Highest ROA (3.1%), lowest P/E NTM (14.9×), lowest EV/EBITDA (11×). ROE 11.5% (second only to Wise). Median P/E 14.9× vs. avg 29.5× shows outlier distortion.
Wise — highest leverage
A/E = 18.5× (vs. median 3.4×). ROE 14.4% but ROA 0.6%. P/Book NTM 11× — operates like a bank.
Negative earners
Square, Robinhood, SoFi, Paytm all have negative ROE. P/E is meaningless — this is ARK’s argument for adjusted EBITDA.
