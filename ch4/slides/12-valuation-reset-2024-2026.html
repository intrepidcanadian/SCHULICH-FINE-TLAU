The 2024–2026 valuation reset.

Private fintech valuations have largely re-rated from the 2021 peak. Stripe and Klarna have begun recovering through tender offers and IPO filings; pure neobanks remain depressed. Public-market multiples have compressed to ~6× EV/Sales for growth and 12–15× P/E for profitable mature fintechs.
