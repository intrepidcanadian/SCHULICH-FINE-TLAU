The valuation toolkit

Price is what you pay. Value is what you get.

Early-stage: angel scorecards (Payne $2M base, Berkus max $2.5M, RFS ±$250K per risk). Growth: VC method works backward from terminal value at 25–35% target IRR, adjusted for survival probability. Mature: market multiples (P/E, EV/Sales, EV/EBITDA) or DCF with WACC ~10%. Banks: P/E and P/Book only. Disruptive fintechs: adjusted EBITDA is one lens — always verify.
