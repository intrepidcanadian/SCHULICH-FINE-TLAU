§4.13b · bank vs digital-bank multiples

Why a Brazilian neobank trades at 2× JPMorgan’s P/E (May 2026).

Slide 10 (Bank Valuation) anchored a regulated bank to P/E + P/Book on Apr 2023 data. Two years later the spread has widened decisively. JPMorgan trades at ~14.8× trailing / ~13.3× forward earnings (May 2026); Nu Holdings sits at ~24-31× trailing / ~20× forward, despite being a lower-cost, higher-ROE digital bank. The classic textbook answer “banks deserve a discount” is now reversed for neobanks: ROE 33% vs 17%, $0.80/customer/month operating cost, and double-digit deposit growth in LatAm push the multiple. The valuation toolkit didn’t change — the underlying unit economics did.
