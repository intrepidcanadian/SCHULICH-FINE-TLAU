§4.11b · Finro Q1 2026 416-company dataset

Q1 2026 fintech multiples: the average is 14.5×. The median is 7.6×. Dispersion is the story.

Slide 14 set the 2025 baseline. Finro's Q1 2026 dataset (416 fintechs across 9 sub-sectors) shows the spread has widened, not converged: the 14.5× average masks a 7.6× median — meaning a handful of WealthTech and Blockchain platforms are dragging the headline up while Lending sits at 2.6×. For the discounted-cash-flow lens you applied in slide 8, this is the year you stop quoting “fintech” multiples in the aggregate. The right comp set lives at the sub-sector level — and AI-native & vertical-SaaS economics pull comps closer to enterprise SaaS (~6–7×) than to legacy lending (~2.6×).
