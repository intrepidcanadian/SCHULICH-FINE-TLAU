§4.7b · mNAV, BTC, & the new asset on the balance sheet

A new valuation problem: corporate BTC treasuries, 2020 → 2026.

Slide 13 introduced tokenized RWA. The mirror trade is "tokenized equity": MicroStrategy (now Strategy) accumulating 818K BTC (~$54B basis), financed mostly through ATM equity sales (~97%). Five years on, the valuation question for analysts is mNAV (market cap ÷ BTC NAV). It hovered ~3× through the 2024 ETF launch and has compressed to sub-1× (~0.65×) by late 2025 as $50B mkt cap traded below ~$77B of BTC at spot — making fresh ATM issuance dilutive.
