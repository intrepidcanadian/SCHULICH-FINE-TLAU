§4.11 · fintech multiples reset 2025

From 2021 mania to 2025 normalisation.

The 2021 fintech multiple peak (Adyen 100× P/E, Block 200×) is gone. Late-2025 multiples reflect the new dual mandate: profitable growth, evident operating leverage. AI-native fintech still trades at premium — but only if Rule of 40 holds.
