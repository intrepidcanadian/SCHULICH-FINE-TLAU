Ten categories · DefiLlama taxonomy

DEXes, lending, liquidity staking — the LEGO stack.

Each category is built from smart contracts. DEXes swap tokens (40% of TVL). Lending lets you borrow against crypto. Liquidity staking pools rewards for staked assets. Yield farming chases the highest APY across protocols.

DEXes (40%)
655 protocols. Curve, Uniswap, PancakeSwap. AMM-based pools replace order books.
Lending (27%)
AAVE, Compound, Anchor. Pool model: lenders deposit, borrowers take collateralized loans.
Liquidity staking (22%)
Lido lets users stake ETH and double-dip the liquidity tokens (stToken) elsewhere. Securities-lending analogue.
CDP (20%)
MakerDAO mints DAI against crypto collateral. Abracadabra mints MIM against interest-bearing tokens.
Bridge tokens solve interoperability: Wrapped Bitcoin (WBTC) is an ERC-20 token backed 1:1 with BTC. It brings Bitcoin liquidity onto Ethereum, where it can be used in any DeFi smart contract.
